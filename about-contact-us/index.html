Our Story

Bloom Aura holds a lot close to our hearts, but mostly, we want to create a space for everyone to feel more creative and at peace. With every product, we aim to bring you a sense of joy and calm; one you can access in both your brightest moments and your darkest.


It all started with a love of stationery and a life-long affection for East-Asian culture. Inspired by visits to the one-of-a-kind stationery stores of Korea and Japan, we wanted to make this incredible + unique culture accessible to everyone across the world.
